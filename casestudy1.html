Project Overview

The Product

Art Gallery is a app that allow users to upload the art as well as can view the artworks and information about them. User can also buy the art piece.

Project information

  • Category: App design
  • Project Duration: March 1 - May 25, 2024

PROBLEM

These days people are using instagram, facebook and other platform to share their artwork to the world. This is convenient to the artist but for the art lover who like to visit and go through art and want to explore more about the artwork have difficult time in searching for art at those platform. Their time is wasted by searching for the art.

THE GOAL

Design an app that allow user to share their art and get more information about the artwork.

ROLE

UX/UI Designer. [UI (user interface), UX (user experience)]

Responsibilities

User research, User persona, Problem statement, User journey map, Competitive audit, Ideation activitie, User flow, Storyboard, Low fidelity wireframe, Low fidelity prototypes, High fidelity mockups, High fidelity prototypes, Usability studies

UNDERSTANDING THE USER

User research: Summary

Conducted interviews and created empathy maps help understand users needs, behaviours and motivations. My goal for conducting primary research is to identified user group. The most artlover were adult, who want to spend time to get information about and write a blog for the art.

DESIGN

Paper wireframe

I sketched paper wireframe of homepage, upload, login and signup page to explore various options for how to organize the information. I made notes on each screen of what I thought worked best, and incorporated those ideas into the digital low fidelity wireframes.



Digital wireframe

Next, I moved to Figma to create digital wireframes for all of the screens. Using a basic design system, I was able to create low fidelity wireframes that were still clean and organized. The homepage serves as a central hub where users can access a quick dashboard. The bottom navigation gives users options to upload the art, share the art, use the search bar, and access the art work. The bottom navigation includes tabs to each of the main pages of the app.

High-Fidelity Prototype

The high-fidelity prototype is the interactive presentation of the product. This is a user flow of the art gallery app which meet user needs to upload and share art work. User can also buy art.


Accessbility Consideration

  1. Used icons to make navigation easier.
  2. Allow disable person to buy art online without going to the store or seller place.
  3. I used headings with different sized text for clear visual hierarchy.

Going Forward

Take away
